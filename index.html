Frequently Asked Questions

Booking Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially for weekend events. Popular dates fill up quickly!
What's included in the booking price?
All packages include tent setup, lighting, sound system, safety equipment, and professional crew. Check each package for specific details.
Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?
Yes! Cancellations 48+ hours in advance receive full refund. Rescheduling is free with 24+ hours notice.

Setup Queries

How long does setup take?
Complete setup takes approximately 45 minutes. We arrive 1 hour before your event start time.
What space do you need?
We need a flat area of at least 18m x 13m with access to standard power outlet. Indoor or outdoor locations work!
Do you provide power?
We need access to a standard 220V power outlet. If unavailable, we can arrange generator rental for additional cost.

Safety & Payment

Is it safe for all ages?
Absolutely! Our equipment meets all safety standards. We provide safety briefings and have trained staff on-site.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfer, and card payments. 50% deposit required to secure booking, balance due on event day.
What happens in bad weather?
Our tent is weather-resistant! Light rain is no problem. For severe weather, we'll work with you to reschedule at no extra cost.
